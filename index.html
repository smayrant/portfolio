About Me

I'm a detail-oriented Front End Developer that loves to learn about new web development concepts/technologies and improve upon my skills and abilities as a developer. I began my coding experience in college but I wasn't quite sure where programming would take me.

After graduating, I ended up trying HTML, CSS, and Javascript and was immediately hooked. Creating user-focused web applications is something that I find fun and thoroughly enjoy doing.

When I'm not consumed with writing code, you can find me surfing the web on sites such as Hackernoon or FreeCodeCamp or brushing up on my skills and learning about the web's newest and most useful technologies. And when I'm not doing anything related to web development, you can find me watching and rooting for any of the Washington DC area sports teams.
